# 🛒 Superstore Sales — Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Objective
Analyze Superstore sales data to identify revenue drivers,
profit leakages and regional performance gaps.

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Sales | $2.30M |
| Total Profit | $286K |
| Profit Margin | 12.47% |
| Total Orders | 5,009 |

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Technology leads with 36.4% revenue share and highest profit margin
- Tables & Bookcases generate net losses — discounts exceed 40%
- West region outperforms all others with best profit-to-sales ratio
- Central region has dangerous profit gap: $501K sales but only $39K profit
- Sales spike every Nov-Dec — Q4 preparation critical for business

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Cap all Furniture discounts at 20% immediately to stop losses
- Replicate West region's product mix strategy in Central and South
- Prepare inventory 2 months before Q4 to capitalize on holiday demand
- Invest in South region — healthier margins but under-penetrated market

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI (DAX, KPI Cards, Slicers, Map Visual)
- Dataset: Sample Superstore (9,994 records)
